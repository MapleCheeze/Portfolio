## Privacy Policy

The privacy of my visitors is paramount. This policy outlines how data is handled on this site. As a security professional, I prioritize data minimization and transparency.

### Log Files
Like most websites, this site uses log files to monitor traffic patterns. This includes IP addresses, browser types, timestamps, and referring pages. This data is used solely for site maintenance and is not linked to personally identifiable information.

### Cookies and Web Beacons
This site uses cookies for basic functionality and analytics. 
* **Analytics:** I use Google Analytics to understand how visitors interact with my research and portfolio.
* **Third Parties:** If you interact with embedded content (like a YouTube video or a GitHub gist), those third parties may set cookies. I have no control over those third-party cookies.

#### Google Analytics (GA4)
Google Analytics reports website trends without identifying individual visitors. You can opt-out by using browser extensions or disabling cookies in your settings.
